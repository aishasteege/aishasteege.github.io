Help: URL Parameters

Collapse All: Append ?collapse-all=true to the URL

Open: To collapse everything except specific groups, append ?collapse-all=true&open=Group1,Group2 to the URL, where Group1 and Group2 are partial matches to the group names. For example, to collapse everything except the groups that have "HDP" in the name, append ?collapse-all=true&open=HDP to the URL.
